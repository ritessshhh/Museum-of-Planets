ABOUT US

Welcome to the Planetary Museum, your virtual portal into the captivating and wondrous expanse of our solar system. While we're based entirely online and are not an official institution, our commitment to providing detailed and accurate information about the celestial bodies that constitute our cosmic neighborhood is unwavering.

The Planetary Museum was originally conceptualized as a passion project, designed to act as a sandbox for honing my web development skills. As a fervent enthusiast of both astronomy and programming, I found the combination of these two interests to be a natural fit. What started as a modest endeavour has now transformed into an extensive online resource, curated carefully with the purpose of sparking curiosity and fostering an appreciation for the marvels of our solar system.

Our museum houses an assortment of exhibitions, each tailored to represent a specific planet in our solar system. We start from the sun-bathed surface of Mercury and journey all the way to the icy frontier of Neptune. Every exhibition is composed of a rich tapestry of facts, intriguing images, and interactive elements to provide a comprehensive understanding of each planet. This virtual museum is not just a place to learn about the cosmos; it's also a testament to my journey in web development. As you explore the intricate details of the planets and their moons, you'll also see the progression of my coding skills, from simple HTML and CSS designs to complex JavaScript functionalities and interactive elements. Behind the scenes of every page is a code that represents countless hours of study, practice, and refinement.

Thank you for joining us on this journey through the cosmos and the realm of coding. Here at the Planetary Museum, our mission is to inspire, educate, and ignite curiosity. We hope that our love for both astronomy and web development shines through and that you leave our website with a newfound or deepened interest in these fascinating fields.
